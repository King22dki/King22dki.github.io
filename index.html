About

Who I am

Hi, I'm Daniel King; an autodidact with a background in Chemical Engineering. By day, I work as an engineering lecturer; by night, my thoughts drift elsewhere...into mathematics, cognitive neuroscience, physics, and philosophy. I have not yet formally worked within probability theory, yet I find myself drawn toward it, particularly in the way higher-dimensional spaces begin to shape our understanding of artificial intelligence. And from time to time, I return to chemistry, not out of obligation, but curiosity...following the quiet evolution of materials, from metal–organic frameworks to the strange and fascinating notion of time crystals.

Perhaps this is the thread that runs through it all...I am not an academic in the conventional sense. I do not move within rigid boundaries, nor do I confine myself to a single discipline. Instead, I find myself exploring, connecting, and revisiting ideas as they emerge. Yet, there is a growing pull toward something more structured, a desire to return to active research...to not only think, but to build, test, and contribute.

It is within this space that Queryous was formed...a venture rooted in the belief that generative AI should not remain confined to a select few, but made accessible within educational institutions. This site, then, becomes an extension of that thinking...a place where projects take shape, where ideas are traced in their early forms, and where ongoing reflections are shared as they unfold.
